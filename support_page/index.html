Account & Data Deletion

We respect your privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your War of Older account and all associated data, you can request data deletion by sending us an email.

Please follow these instructions to ensure your request is processed quickly:

  • Send an email to suporte@warofolder.com.
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  • Include the Email Address associated with your account or your Player ID (Public ID found in your profile).

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. Once processed, all your progress, coins, and VIP status will be permanently lost. The process is completed within 3 to 5 business days.
