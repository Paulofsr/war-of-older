Frequently Asked Questions

General & Gameplay

War of Older is a real-time online strategy game based on Tic-Tac-Toe. It features 5 unique board variants, global leaderboards, multiplayer duels, and offline modes — available on both Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store).

From the main screen, tap Play Online, select your preferred board, and the app will automatically match you with an opponent. You need an active internet connection for online duels.

Yes! War of Older includes a full offline mode where you can play against a friend on the same device, or against the AI. Simply select Local Play from the main menu.

The Top 50 Ranking is updated periodically (every 60 minutes). You earn score points by winning online matches. The ranking screen shows a live countdown to the next update, along with a refresh button when new data is available.

Purchases & Subscriptions

The in-app store offers:

  • 🪙Coin Packs — Handful (10), Sack (30), and Chest (100) of Coins
  • ⭐PRO Account — removes ads and unlocks exclusive features
  • 👑VIP Account — full VIP status with all PRO benefits and premium cosmetics

To restore previously purchased non-consumable items (PRO Account, VIP Account) on a new device:

  1. 1 Open the app and go to the Store
  2. 2 Scroll to the bottom of the store screen
  3. 3 Tap "Restore Purchases"
  4. 4 Sign in with your original Apple ID or Google account when prompted
Coin packs are consumable items and cannot be restored. Only PRO Account and VIP Account are restorable.

First, try the Restore Purchases button in the store. If the issue persists, please contact us at suporte@warofolder.com with:

  • › Your account email or Player ID
  • › A screenshot or order number from your purchase receipt (Google Play or App Store)

Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the app — this is the fastest method:

  1. 1 Open the app and tap the menu icon (top-right corner)
  2. 2 Tap Logout
  3. 3 In the dialog that appears, tap the red "Delete Account" option
  4. 4 Enter your password to confirm
  5. 5 Confirm — your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

Alternatively, you can send a deletion request by email:

  • › Email: suporte@warofolder.com
  • › Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • › Include your account email or Player ID
⚠️ Account deletion is irreversible. All your progress, coins, PRO/VIP status, and match history will be permanently and immediately deleted. This action cannot be undone.

We collect minimal data: game usage statistics (anonymized), device type, language, and optionally your email for account authentication. We do not collect full names, phone numbers, or government IDs.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Go to your Profile screen within the app, tap on your username, and edit it directly. Username changes take effect immediately.

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You will receive a password reset link. If you don't receive it within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

If you still have trouble, contact us at suporte@warofolder.com.
